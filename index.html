About us

A community built to learn, build, and grow together.

The AI/ML Community is an inclusive group of 700+ students, software engineers and AI enthusiasts. We share learning resources, host study sessions, and collaborate on real-world machine learning projects.

We welcome members of all genders, backgrounds, and skill levels — from absolute beginners to experienced practitioners. If you're curious about AI, you belong here.
